Welcome to computers101, this will teach you alot about how computers work. Computers are made up of these tiny parts called "Transistors" and transistors are like buttons but instead of physicly pushing the button you use electricity to push the button. On a transistor theres 2 inputs and 1 output, the 2 inputs are called the "Collector" and the "Base" and the 1 output is called the "Emmiter", the base is like the button and the collecter and emmiter are like the inputs and outputs of the button.
